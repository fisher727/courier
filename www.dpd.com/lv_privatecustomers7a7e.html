Packaging tips

How to pack your shipment?

All you need to know in 4 easy steps:

  • 1

    Parcel content

    Parcel's content must be packed so that in time of transportation it would not move and nothing could fall out of the parcel. In order to ensure that, use packaging materials (bubble wrap, corrugated cardboard, cotton, special fixing foam, etc.). If shipment contains an object with sharp edges, it must be packed so that it could not damage or tear packaging.

  • 2

    Packaging

    In order to protect your parcel during transportation, it is very important to choose such packaging that is suitable for chosen content and its weight. Important thing is also to take into account maximum weight and size restrictions.

    > Shipment packaging
    > Maximum weights and dimensions

  • 3

    Consignee's address

    Always make sure that the consignee address on the label is clearly readable and correct. Check if the postcode is accurate. Don't forget to provide consignee's name and telephone number. 

    > More information about consignee's address

  • 4

    Parcel label

    In order to ship your parcel, you must also have parcel label with consignees address on it. Please pay attention to have only one parcel label on the packaging. If on the packaging are several parcel labels, the parcel might be sent to the wrong recipient.

    > More about double labels
