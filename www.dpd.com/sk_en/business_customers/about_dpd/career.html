About DPD
Career

Good prospects for your career.

Our employees are the cornerstone of our success. DPD is constantly on the lookout for new, motivated and well qualified employees who can make a contribution to our company and help us to maintain the peak performance which our customers expect. As a parcel service provider with worldwide operations we can offer our staff a variety of interesting tasks and good entry and development opportunities.

In addition to attractive vacancies for applicants with professional experience and for university/college graduates with degrees in business studies, IT, logistics and engineering, DPD also offers practical training opportunities. Our training courses in IT engineering, IT systems sales and commerce provide young people with an entry into professional life. As a parcel service we naturally also train freight forwarding and CEP specialists. We also offer work-experience places for trainees.
