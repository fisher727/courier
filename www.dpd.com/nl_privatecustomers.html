Pickup

Organising at your convenience.

A Pickup parcelshop is the best solution if you want to receive or return parcels easily. Thanks to our extensive network from Pickup parcelshops in the Netherlands, Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Croatia, Czech Republic,  Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Portugal, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, Switserland and United Kingdom, you can always rely on outstanding service and flexibility. We have approximately 750 Pickups, so there is always one nearby. 

How can we help you?

  • Shipping: affordable and simple parcel shipping
  • Receiving: let Pickup parcelshop deliver your parcel
  • Returning: send your parcel via a Pickup parcelshop

Do you send an occasional parcel? Or do you send several parcels a week? Then you already in the right place for our shipping service. You will find more information on www.pakkettenversturen.nl.

Returns

Easily return via a Pickup parcelshop.

Are you unhappy with your order? And would you like to return it? If so, do this easily via a Pickup parcelshop.

  • 1

    Simple returns

    DPD makes it easy for you to return your order. Your parcel will be speedily returned to the shipper with our returns service. Possibly even at no extra cost for you. Just check with your supplier for the options.

  • 2

    Affixing the return label

    There is usually a return label with your order. In some cases you may have to request one from the sender, which will then be emailed to you. Print this label, stick the return label onto your parcel, and it is ready for shipping.

  • 3

    Handing in a parcel

    Take your parcel to a Pickup parcelshop. We have approximately 750. Click here to see where your nearest Pickup parcelshop is.
