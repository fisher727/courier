DPDMyparcel

Freedom is easy
redirection of parcels.

Navigate your parcel - free of charge - even if you don't happen to be at home. You specify online – right round the clock, seven days a week – where or when we make the delivery.

All the changes you can make at a glance:

  • Select a neighbour. And we deliver to your neighbour.
  • Give permission to deposit. We leave it where you wish. 
  • Select a Pickup parcelshop. Collect the parcel any time.
  • Change the place. We deliver to your office, for example. 
  • Change the day. And we deliver on the day you specify.

Predict

We tell you, when we arrive.

With Predict you know in advance when DPD courier will arrive. On the day of delivery of the parcel the recipient is notified in email fixing the time of delivery. By this email the recipient is informed of a 1-hour time span within which the consignment will be delivered. Time window is generated from system and works with historical data about shipping. Predict is available for consignees of our contrated partners ordered this optional service.

Why DPD?

Our parcels are well received.

By the environment, too.

  • Responsibility

    Each package delivered via DPD is carbon neutral. Transportation of your shipments with DPD does not leave a footprint on the environment. More information can be found here.

  • Change of delivery

    Not at home? No problem. Change the day and place of delivery at the click of a mouse. 

  • Safety

    Your parcel is in safe hands with us and is well insured. Includes insurance of up to 520 € per parcel for domestic and road, and up to 20 USD/kg in international air shipment.
