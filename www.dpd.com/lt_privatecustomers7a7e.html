Pickup network

Bring your parcels to our Pickup network

Pickup parcelshops and lockers is a convenient alternative for sending and receiving parcels. Our Pickup network solution brings you more freedom - you will no longer have to wait for a courier to send a parcel.

Pickup network was created for your convenience:

  • Possibility to choose the most convenient location to send a parcel;
  • Longer opening hours, and often opened on the weekend;
  • Freedom - does not require being at home / work on agreed time; 
  • Notification about parcel delivery by SMS or email;
  • Security – parcels are insured;

Packaging tips

How to pack parcels which make everybody happy - including us.

All you need to know in 4 easy steps:

  • 1

    Parcel content

    There are some things that can't be shipped, because they are either dangerous or illegal, e.g. cigarettes to your friend in the UK, mum's favorite flowerpot with orchids or that cute little puppy for your niece. You can find the full list of these things in our General Terms and Conditions.

    Your parcel does not consists of any of these items? Great, you can move to the next step!

  • 2

    Packaging

    We truly appreciate when the packaging is firm and robust. Parcel's content must be packed so that in time of transportation it would not move and nothing could fall out of the parcel. In order to ensure that, use packaging materials (bubble wrap, corrugated cardboard, cotton, fixing foam, etc.). For more packaging tips, click here.

    We can handle packages up to 31.5 kg, but we only transport parcels up to 20 kg from Pickup network. We cannot fit huge packages into our vans, so please check our max dimensions for esiunta.lt users and Pickup network users.

  • 3

    Consignee's address

    In order to deliver your parcel on time and without trouble we need a complete and clear contact information: first name, surname, address, post code and the phone number of the consignee. Please add the name of the company when the parcel is sent to the consignee's work address.

    If you use esiunta.lt do not forget to print the parcel label with all the contact details. If you use Pickup parcelshops, fill in the manual label handed to you by a Pickup employee. If you use Pickup Station lockers, enter contact information on the screen. 

  • 4

    Parcel label

    The label is stuck on the upper surface. Please make sure that the parcel label is not covered in any way – either by tape, string, markings or other stickers.

    It's no problem if you use a package which has already been used before, but please make sure that any existing and confusing address information or old barcodes are removed. This will make sure that your parcel arrives where you want it to go.
