Current fuel surcharge and toll fee

The domestic transport

The fuel surcharge in March 2019 is 7%. Toll fee is 0,5 CZK (VAT not included) for each kg of weight of the transported parcel.

 

The international transport

The fuel surcharge in March 2019 is 9,5% for road transport and 23% for air transport. Toll fee is 0,50 CZK (VAT not included) for each kg of weight of the transported parcel.
