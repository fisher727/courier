Depots

Your parcels go to 1 central hub and to 1 of our 14 depots

Thanks to the DPDgroup, we have an interconnected network of depots and hubs in Europe. In the Czech Republic, we have built 14 depots and one central hub which is situated near Prague.

The majority of the picked up parcels is taken to the central hub first, then to one of our regional depots and then it goes directly to the hands of the consignee.

In our depots, you can pick up your parcels within the Pickup point network, as well as leave your parcels there to have them shipped. In the table below, you can find addresses of all our depots with links to Google Maps to find them easily.
