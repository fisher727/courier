International

How much does international transport cost?

The price depends on the weight of the parcel and the chosen service. There is a toll fee and fuel surcharge which changes every month (not included in the price).

Are you interested in a long term cooperation?

Well, we must say that is great news. If you're serious about it, send us the approximate amount of parcels for a month, their approximate weight and destination. If you do all that, we might be able to offer you the most a suitable price.

 

You can find out the price by these means:

  • Via our online form
  • By dropping us a few lines at [email protected]
  • You can always give us a ring at 225 373 373

Current fuel surcharge and toll fee

The fuel surcharge in October 2019 is 9,5% for road transport and 23% for air transport. Toll fee is 0,50 CZK (VAT not included) for each kg of weight of the transported parcel.

Road transport

  • Handling the customs services for the consignee: 900 CZK
  • Handling the customs services for both the consignee and the consignor: 1100 CZK

 

Air transport

  • Handling the customs services for the consignee: paid by the consignee
  • Handling the customs services paid by the consignor:
    • Issuance of the Export Declaration for 4 units: included in the transport prices
    • Export Declaration for a single extra unit: 200 CZK
    • Issuance of extra customs documents (EUR1, A.TR): 600 CZK/Declaration document

The surcharge on customs from countries outside of EU to the Czech Republic is 550 CZK per parcel.

The fuel surcharge depends on the average diesel price (detected by the Czech Statistical Office and published every Friday on the CSO website www.czso.cz). We always state the fuel surcharge and the toll fee separately on our invoices. We charge these fees to all services that are directly linked to transport.
