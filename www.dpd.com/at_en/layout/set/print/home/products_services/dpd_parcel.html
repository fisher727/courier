Easy, Fast and Reliable

With DPD PARCEL you benefit from totally reliable and highly efficient shipping solutions for the trouble-free distribution of your goods. Tailor-made for parcel shipping, easy to process, with short standard delivery times and outstanding value for money - enabling you to concentrate fully on your core business. Small-scale business shippers and private consignors do also have the possiblity to take their parcels to a DPD ParcelShop nearby.

DPD PARCEL means that parcels are picked up from the consignor and delivered to the consignee as fast as possible - within one day for domestic shipments, one to four days internationally.

DPD offers a variety of service options to back up your professional parcel shipping: ranging from the environmentally-friendly re-usable packaging system SHUTTLE-BOX® via our customer-friendly DPD returns logistics right down to higher insurance. You can take advantage of these individually or in combination to put together exactly the service mix you require.

Your local DPD depot will be happy to provide you with further details!
