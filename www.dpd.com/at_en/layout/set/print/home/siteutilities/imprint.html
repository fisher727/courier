Activities:

DPD - Austria's leading parcel service - ships more than 50,5 million parcels a year throughout Austria, with a well-designed portfolio of products and services providing the right solution for every occasion. We make sure that your parcels get to their destination safely, fast and reliably - regardless of whether it's in Austria or in a further 230 countries/territories worldwide. Founded in 1988 as Austria's first parcel service, DPD is based on the logistics infrastructure of its shareholders - Austria's leading freight forwarders Gebrüder Weiss, Lagermax and Schachinger. A workforce of 1,700 with a total of 1,200 vehicles are in operation round the clock on your behalf to make sure that your parcel reaches its destination as fast as possible. 14,000 Austrian companies benefit every day from our maximum flexibility and can always rely on the highest standards of service and quality.
