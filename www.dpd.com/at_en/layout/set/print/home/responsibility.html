We carry our Parcel with Responsibility.

As one of the leading companies in the transport and logistics industry, DPD is aware of its responsibility. Not only in relation to the environment, but also to society as a whole and the financial and social needs of our own employees.

Consequently, DPD make responsible management a strategic focus. This is translated into action across the international DPD network through hundreds of projects and initiatives. DPD assumes responsibility in three central areas.

 

 

Environment

  • We improve our carbon footprint.
  • We reduce our energy consumption.
  • We separate and recycle our rubbish.
  • We use vehicles with alternative drive systems

Employees

  • We promote health, safety and well-being in the workplace.
  • We develop the strengths and talents of each individual.
  • Variety and diversity are important to us.

Community

  • We support people and organisations in challenging situations.
  • We work with local interest groups to develop optimum logistics solutions.
  • We raise awareness of responsibility to society.
