Our packaging tips – safety first…

The right packaging always depends on the type, size, weight and strength of your shipment pieces.

 

  • Pack compact – always choose the smallest possible box size
  • Heavy below, light above – pad adequately
  •  Avoid cavities and contact with the side panels
  •  You shall not save filling material – the distance to the top should be 5 - 10 cm.
  •  Safely closed – easily with the integrated closing flap
  • Please always attach the DPD parcel label (address sticker), easy to read, on the biggest surface
