Depot

Your DPD depot. Never very far away.

This is the right place for you if you regularly ship large quantities of parcels and are a contract customer, because DPD has one of the most comprehensive integrated transport networks connecting the world's main international business regions. This means that we can ensure trouble-free processing at all times, backed up by a smooth flow of information and goods – the basis for perfect shipping solutions, even for highly complex tasks in the area of cross-border logistics.

To find your local DPD depot you select the required country in the depot finder and enter the postcode of the town in which your company is located.
