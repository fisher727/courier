DPD in Austria

DPD - Austria's leading private parcel service - ships 50,5 million parcels annually. A well-designed range of products and services offers just the right solution for every occasion. We make sure that your parcels get to their destination safely, fast and reliably - regardless of whether it's in Austria or in a further 230 countries/territories worldwide.

Founded in 1988 as Austria's first parcel service, DPD is based on the logistics infrastructure of its shareholders - Austria's leading freight forwarders Gebrüder Weiss, Lagermax and Schachinger. A workforce of approx. 1,700 with a total of 1,200 vehicles are in operation round the clock on your behalf to make sure that your parcel reaches its destination as fast as possible. 14,000 Austrian companies benefit every day from our maximum flexibility and can always rely on the highest standards of service and quality. We set the benchmarks - both domestic and international.

Top levels of performance and quality - these are what you benefit from with DPD. We work on the guiding principle that parcels are the property of a consignor or a consignee. They are guests in our system and to make sure they are treated like guests all our operations are based on detailed organisational instructions - our quality assurance system (ISO 9001).
