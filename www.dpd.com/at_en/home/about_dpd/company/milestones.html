Milestones

Success in succession – DPD milestones

From Austria’s first privately owned parcel service provider to one of the leading international parcel delivery company. Discover DPD’s success story, we invite you to a journey through time with us…

 

30 years of DPD Austria at a glance.

2018/2017 

2016

2015

One group, one symbol! 

Launch of the Europe-wide unified brand appearance

 

2014

DPD goes 2C!

 

DPD enters the private customer segment and opens Austrian-wide more than 600 Pickup parcel shops. 

 

2014 there are 17,000 Pickup parcel shops all over Europe.

 

2013 - 2008

Parcel records & increasing turnover

 

2013 – Austria’s leading private parcel service company turns 25

 

2010 – Despite a tense economic situation, DPD Austria records a parcel volume of 37 mio.

 

2008 – Turnover increases to 142.9 mio. euro

 

2003 – Continuing upward trend. DPD Austria ships more than 27 mio. parcels

2002

The speedy parcel!

 

As the first parcel service company in Austria, DPD provides a 24 hour scheduled delivery time to German cities.

 

2001

New record!

 

 

Within one day, the DPD system shippes 1 mio. parcels into 220 countries and territories.

1999 - 1994

New challenges!

 

1999 – New depots in Salzburg, Upper Austria and Styria

 

1996 – Opening of the central HUB in Hörsching

 

1994 – APS Austria Paket System starts cross-boarder parcel shipping
