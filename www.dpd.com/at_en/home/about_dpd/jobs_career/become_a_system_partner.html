Become a system Partner

Become a DPD system partner…

…and enter an industry with future! Benefit from the growing parcel market in Austria!

In 2018 DPD moved about 50.5 mio parcels for business and private customers. On that note, we are the biggest privately owned parcel service in Austria and the strongest provider within the B2B segment.*

 

We are an attractive partner:

  • Strong presence – 14 depots, 1 HUB, 3 City HUBs and 1400 Pickup Parcelshops
  • Strongest network – daily 1200 tours from Monday to Saturday to each address in Austria
  • More than 3 decades of market experience – first private parcel service in Austria
  • Internationally strong – No. 2 in Europe**, delivery in 230 countries and territories, biggest street network in Austria

 

The parcel market in Austria grows constantly. More and more goods are ordered online and arrive via parcel at the recipient. Companies expand, open up branches and establish online shops.

Benefit from this positive market development and become a DPD system partner in Austria – a safe and promising business!

 

We are on the road for our customers with 250 linehauls and 1200 shipping tours, as well as a fleet of 1200 vans.

 

As a DPD system partner you should provide:

 

  • a registered office in Austria
  • know-how and expertise in the parcel service segment
  • a corresponding capacity of delivery vehicles
  • reliable parcel deliverers with a perfect reputation and good German language skills
  • great flexibility
  • great commitment

 

Successful partnership.

We stand for fair partnerships, economic thinking, communication as equals as well as high performance and quality levels.

Our success as Austria’s biggest private parcel service is not at last a result of good relations with customers, employees and system partners. Thereby we consider a central principle: all parcels belong to a sender or a recipient, they are guests in our system.

 

Become a DPD system partner now!

If you want to grow with us, than we are looking forward to your contact.

 

* Study KEP Branchenradar, Feb. 2019 | ** dpdgroup
