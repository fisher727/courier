Become a parcel driver

Working in Austria! Become part of about 1.700 DPD logistic experts.

 

Job opportunity as parcel driver in Austria!

 

DPD cooperates with a lot of Austrian small transport contractors, who get assigned with the delivery and collection of the customer parcels. We will forward your application as parcel driver to appropriate system partners.

(Privacy policy)

 

The parcel logistic booms – hence the industry is constantly in search for active enhancement.

Requirements:

  • You work independently and enjoy being on the road
  • Customer satisfaction is your number one priority
  • Your easily lift and carry 31,5 kg parcels
  • The handling of technical devices comes off easily to you
  • Your have good german skills and can have a conversations without problems
  • You have a compulsory school leaving qualification and a driving license B

 

Looking for a save employment in Austria? Provide us your data not binding, so we can forward your application to appropriate system partners.
