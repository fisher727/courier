My parcel has been delivered to an alternative delivery address.

The delivery time of your parcel depends on the chosen shipping solution and the country of destination. The shipping within Austria takes regularly one working day (Monday - Friday). Within Europe, your parcel will reach its destination within 1 to 4 working days. Recipients worldwide will receive their parcel within max. 10 days.

Our Delivery Time Calculator shows you the delivery time of over 230 countries/territories of destination.

Should you already have redirected you parcel with the myDPD.at, we deliver your parcel either one day after the planned delivery ore at the from you defined working day (Monday - Friday).

Our business service Primetime allows commercial senders a Saturday delivery.

Recipients of Predict parcels have the possibility to redirect their parcel, with myDPD.at, already before the first delivery. Ahead of the planned delivery time, you will receive an email with a 2 hour delivery time frame, in which you can expect your parcel. You also are able to track your parcel on screen in real time with the feature DPD Live-Tracking. 
Recipients of parcels without the Predict feature will be able to redirect their parcel, on myDPD.at, after the first delivery attempt. For this you need the parcel label number, that can be found on the left parcel notification card/parcel information.

You are at any time – already at the preparation of the shipping label – able to enter any desired destination for your parcel (Attention: please don't use post office boxes ore Packstationen as a delivery address). For the ones who want to receive the parcel even when they are not at home: Give us a permission to leave parcel without signature and we deliver the parcel to a save place of your choice.

You are the director of your parcel, with myDPD.at.
Not at home? No problem! Navigate your parcel - free of charge - even if you don't happen to be at home. You specify online – right round the clock, seven days a week – where and when we make the delivery.

All the changes you can make at a glance:

  • Give permission to deposit. We leave the parcel where you specify. 
  • Select a Pickup Parcelshop. You collect the parcel when it suits you.
  • Change the place. We deliver the parcel to your office, for example. 
  • Heres to good neighborliness. Direct your parcel to your favorite neighbor. 
  • Change the day. And we deliver on the day you specify.
  • Refusal of the delivery. You have changed your mind? No problem – we will send your parcel right back.
