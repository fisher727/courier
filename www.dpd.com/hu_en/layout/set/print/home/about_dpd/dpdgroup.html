Introducing DPDgroup.

With a daily volume of 3 million parcels the international DPDgroup is the second-largest international parcel delivery network in Europe. At more than 800 different locations a workforce of 26,000 employees operates on behalf of 360,000 customers, providing uncomplicated, convenient and flexible B2B and B2C parcel shipping. One example of this is the company's Predict service, which is unique within the parcels industry, together with its online map-based Live-Tracking function, a range of options for redirecting parcels and a one hour delivery window. With more than 500 daily cross-border transport operations covering 35 countries DPDgroup has the best road network in Europe. Throughout the continent 16,000 parcel shops are closely networked, all of them with a uniform infrastructure, the same IT equipment and harmonised products and services.

DPDgroup is the international parcel delivery network of GeoPost, a wholly-owned subsidiary of France's La Poste. In the year 2014 GeoPost posted sales of 4.9 billion euros, which represents growth of 13.3% on the year before.

In 20 European nations the DPD brand is an everyday sight on the country's roads. In March 2015 we adopted a new corporate identity which reflects our status as a modern and innovative parcel shipping service. This new brand identity also clearly profiles our position within Europe because we have brought together the DPD, Chronopost, SEUR and Interlink Express brands under the newly-created umbrella of DPDgroup, highlighting the fact that they are all members of the same strong group.
