Reach your customers worldwide

DPD CLASSIC is perfect if you need dependable, attractively-priced parcel services to European countries or further.

 

We have a range of useful tips and tools for your international parcel shipping:

 

  • Check out current parcel delivery times here to find out how long your parcel will take to reach its destination.
  • Our shipping planner will help you to prepare and synch your international parcel shipments.
  • Use our online form to draw up your commercial invoice: simply fill it in and print it out on your company stationery.
  • We have also compiled all the information you need to ensure your exports to non-EU countries reach your customers smoothly.

 

DPD CLASSIC shipments are delivered Monday to Friday. Please note that surcharges apply to a number of islands and regions in Europe (to cover ferry costs and the additional costs we need to incur in order to reach hard-to-access locations).

 

DPD CLASSIC benefits include:

  • Efficient and secure shipping to over 170 countries worldwide.
  • Fast standard deliveries: 1-5 days in Europe, approx. 3-8 days worldwide.
  • Up to 8,33 SDR/kg insurance per parcel as standard, additional insurance as an optional extra.
  • Your shipments are AEO-F certified, meaning they clear through customs faster.
  • Online shipping status updates (I.O.D.).
  • Simple shipment processing: only one parcel label for all destination countries.

 

Additional benefits in European shipping:

 

  • Online P.O.D.s.
  • Road transport generates eight times less CO2 than airfreight. Around 80% of DPD's deliveries in Europe travel by road.

 

Additional benefits in worldwide shipping:

 

  • Your parcels receive SPX security status (secure for passenger, all-cargo and all-mail aircraft) before they are loaded as airfreight, at no extra cost to you.
