About DPD

As a leading provider of standard and express parcel services DPD operates day in day out on behalf of thousands of satisfied customers. We are part of the international DPD network and are also experts in our own domestic market. Communication is a priority at DPD – with customers, journalists and potential employees. As a company we are also very much aware of our social responsibilities.
