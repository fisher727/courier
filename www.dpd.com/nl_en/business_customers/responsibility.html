Responsibility

"We believe we can make a positive impact by focusing our effort on areas closely related to our core business."

We believe in being a responsible partner to the people, businesses and communities we work closely with. 

DrivingChange™ is our commitment to Corporate Social Responsibility. It is our commitment to both reduce our environmental footprint, and drive social and economic improvements in the communities that we serve. We believe we can achieve this through DrivingChange™.

Our approach is intended to be pragmatic. Our actions are meant to be the most efficient possible. That is why we are focusing on areas where we can make the biggest difference within parcel delivery. We focus our CSR ambitions on four axes closely related to our business and on which we can have a direct, positive impact.
