Pickup network

Pickup points

Pickup network is a convenient and safe alternative for sending and receiving parcels. There are already more than 95 Pickup points all over Latvia. 

Options offered by Pickup network:

  • Shipping - ship your parcel to any place in Latvia, Baltics and Europe
  • Receiving - make your orders in e-shops and have them delivered to Pickup points
