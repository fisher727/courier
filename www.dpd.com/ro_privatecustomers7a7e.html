DPD Standard

Safe and flexible solutions

DPD standard courier service for shipments across Romania.

 

Characteristics:

 

  • Delivery within 24 hours between DPD offices and automats;
  • 24-48 hours “Door-to-Door”*delivery; 
  • 100% country coverage;
  • 2 delivery attempts;
  • Track & Trace online;
  • Proof of delivery - available online ;
  • Liability limit - 10 times the transport price; 
  • Free of charges redirecting for addresses within the same DPD office;
  • Maximum number of parcels per shipment - 10 parcels;
  • Maximum weight allowed per parcel – 40kg ;
  • Payment at destination; 

*except for the sites of the Danube Delta

Additional services:

 

  • Cash on Delivery/COD;
  • Return of documents (ROD);
  • Shipment exchange (SWAP);
  • Original proof of delivery;
  • Extended liability;
  • Return of undelivered parcels;
  • POS/MPOS payment;
  • Shipment redirection to another locality

Choose the best option for you

• The DPD Standard 24 hour tariff is applied for deliveries between DPD Collect offices and automats.
• For DPD automats the maximum number of parcels allowed in one shipment is 1 parcel.
• For DPD automats, the maximum weight allowed per parcel is 20kg, and the maximum dimension is 60 x 35 x 37 cm
• For DPD offices, the maximum allowed weight per parcel is 31.5 kg.
• DPD Offices not receive more than 30 parcels/day from one sender and the total weight will not exceed
500kg.
• Prices in RON, VAT and fuel surcharge 5% included.

Packing tips

How to pack parcels which make everybody happy - including us.

Packing a parcel the right way isn't rocket science, but there are always a few details that can be optimised. This is how you do it:

  • 1

    Packaging

    Sending parcels with DPD is a very simple process. Your package must be packed properly for transport and be adequate for processing by sorted bands. And to make sure that all the contents remain intact it's best to use cushioning material: soft padding for light goods and harder padding for heavy stuff. Pack the goods in such a way that nothing can move around inside the parcel. That increases stability. Job done!

  • 2

    Parcel label

    Shipping label must be stuck on the top of the package for it to be visible. Please make sure that the parcel label is not covered in any way – either by string, markings or other stickers. Everything perfect.

  • 3

    Parcel contents

    There are some things that can't be shipped, because they are either dangerous or illegal: for example grandfather's old pistol, mum's string of real pearls or that cute little puppy for your grandchild. All the things that aren't permitted are listed in our General Terms and Conditions. If you aren't sure it's best to check. So, that just about covers everything. Many thanks – your DPD Service.
